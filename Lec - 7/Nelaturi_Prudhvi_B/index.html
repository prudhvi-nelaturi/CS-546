My Travel Destinations

Pocono Mountains in Pennsylvania

So, this last spring I went to Pocono Mountains for trailing and it was so amazing. We along with 5 other friends rented a house from VRBO, a car from Enterprise.

It's a two days trip. We went to Jim Thorpe, Stone Park, VineYard in Pennsylvania. We did hiking in one of trails where we followed the water flow in the woods.

  1. Hickory Run State Park: The park is spread across the Pocono Mountains. The park is easily accessible from Interstate 476 and Interstate 80
  2. Jim Thorpe: Jim Thorpe is a town in eastern Pennsylvania. The Mauch Chunk Museum and Cultural Center details the area’s coal mining history, and the life of the town’s namesake, Native American athlete Jim Thorpe.
  3. Pocono Raceway: Pocono Raceway, also known as The Tricky Triangle, is a superspeedway located in the Pocono Mountains in Long Pond, Pennsylvania
